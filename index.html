How the modes differ in practice

Ironman
  • • Complete self-sufficiency from day one
  • • No Grand Exchange or player trading (except bonds in some cases)
  • • Focus on efficient skilling, questing, and bossing for supplies
  • • Official hiscores & support in RuneLite
Bronzeman COMMUNITY
  • • Start restricted like Ironman
  • • Unlock an item permanently the first time you obtain it (drop, craft, or PK)
  • • After unlock → buy/sell that item freely on the GE
  • • Ideal for building powerful PK accounts over time
Requires RuneLite Bronzeman plugin for enforcement

Efficient Early Quest Order

This recommended order respects level requirements, gives big XP rewards early, and minimizes painful travel. Quests are clustered geographically where possible and build on each other.

# Quest Recommended Stats Why Now? Key Rewards Notes
1 Cook's Assistant None Very fast starter quest in Lumbridge. Good early Cooking XP. Cooking XP, quest points Perfect first quest. No travel needed.
2 Rune Mysteries None Unlocks Aubury's rune shop + early Runecrafting access. Still in Lumbridge/Varrock area. Runes, quest points, RC access Do right after Cook's. Very quick.
3 Waterfall Quest None (combat helpful) Do this early. Gives massive Attack + Strength XP. Makes every combat quest after much easier. ~13k Attack & Strength XP, Glarial's amulet High priority. Big combat boost early.
4 Tree Gnome Village Some combat recommended Unlocks Spirit Tree teleport. Good combat XP. Moves you toward better travel. Spirit Tree access, quest points Do after Waterfall for easier combat.
5 The Grand Tree Tree Gnome Village completed Unlocks Gnome Gliders + more travel options. Good follow-up to Tree Gnome Village. Gnome Glider network, quest points Geographic efficiency — same area.
6 Lost City Some crafting + combat Unlocks Zanaris + Dragon dagger. Good combat XP and a strong early weapon (especially useful for Bronzeman). Dragon dagger, Zanaris access Bronzeman: Dragon dagger is a great early unlock.
7 Fairytale I - Growing Pains Lost City recommended Starts the fairy ring questline. Important for long-term travel efficiency. Quest points, progress toward fairy rings Do this before major Ardougne content.
8 Fairytale II - Cure a Queen Fairytale I + some combat Completes fairy ring access. One of the biggest early QoL upgrades. Full fairy ring network Major travel unlock. Do this relatively early.
9 Fight Arena After Waterfall (easier with stats) Good combat XP + unlocks. Better done after Waterfall's big XP boost. Combat XP, quest points Geographic + level friendly after Waterfall.
10 Priest in Peril Some combat Unlocks Morytania. Required for many later quests and content. Morytania access, quest points Opens up a whole new region.
11 Nature Spirit Priest in Peril completed Unlocks Mort Myre Swamp + good early prayer training method. Mort Myre access, prayer XP method Strong follow-up to Priest in Peril.
12 Recipe for Disaster (Key Subquests) Varies by subquest Major goal. Work through subquests as you meet requirements. Leads to Barrows Gloves. Barrows Gloves + many useful rewards Biggest early/mid milestone. Do subquests as you can.
Tip: After getting Fairy Rings (step 8), travel becomes much less painful. This is why we prioritize it before heavy Ardougne content.

Combat Training by Mode

Different Ironman modes have very different risk tolerances. Here are tailored combat training recommendations.

Hardcore Ironman Safe Training
Sand Crabs / Ammonite Crabs
Early Game • Very Safe
Recommended

Location: Fossil Island or Crabclaw Isle

Safe Spot: Stand behind rocks or use the island safespot. Very limited aggro range.

Why Safe for HCIM: Extremely low damage. Excellent for long AFK sessions with almost zero risk.

Requirements: Bone Voyage quest (or access to Crabclaw Isle)
Experiments (Safespot)
Early-Mid Game • Very Safe

Location: Stronghold of Security (4th floor)

Safe Spot: Corner or limited aggro position where only 1-2 can reach you.

Why Safe for HCIM: Very consistent and low-risk. Great for safely building Defence and HP.

Requirements: Access to Stronghold of Security
Flesh Crawlers
Early Game • Safe

Location: Edgeville Dungeon (level 2)

Safe Spot: Use the ladder area or wall to limit aggro to 1-2 at a time.

Why Good: Decent XP + herb/rune drops. Manageable risk with proper positioning.

Requirements: Some Defence levels recommended
Moss Giants (Safespot)
Early-Mid Game

Location: Varrock Sewers or near Fishing Guild

Safe Spot: Use rocks/walls to limit how many can attack you.

Why Useful: Good training while collecting big bones and mossy keys safely.

Solid option for building toward mid-game content.
Hill Giants
Early Game • Safe

Location: Edgeville Dungeon or near the Fishing Guild

Safe Spot: Use corners or obstacles to fight 1 at a time safely.

Why Safe for HCIM: Predictable and easy to control. Good for early combat training.

Great for collecting big bones safely.
Rock Crabs (Rellekka)
Early-Mid Game • Very Safe

Location: Rellekka coastline (near the rock crabs)

Safe Spot: Position yourself so only 1-2 crabs can reach you.

Why Safe for HCIM: Extremely AFK-friendly with very low damage output.

Requires some travel but very safe once set up.
Early Slayer Tasks (Safe)
Early Game • Safe

Recommended Tasks: Cave Bugs, Cave Slimes, Lizards, Birds

Safe Spot Strategy: Use terrain or safespots on most early tasks. Avoid multi-combat areas when possible.

Why Good for HCIM: Builds Slayer while keeping risk very low.

Focus on tasks with natural safespots or low damage output.
Gemstone Crab
Early-Mid Game • Extremely AFK
Highly Recommended

Location: Varlamore (Gemstone Crab area)

Safe Spot: Stand in the designated safe area where the crab attacks you automatically with very low effort required.

Why Excellent for HCIM: One of the best AFK combat training methods currently available. Extremely low risk and attention required.

Requirements: Access to Varlamore
HCIM Tip: Always have an emergency teleport (Ring of dueling, Games necklace, or home teleport) ready. Never risk content you're not confident surviving.

Away From Keyboard (AFK) Skilling

Low-effort training methods perfect for Ironmen who want to train while doing other things. Click a skill tab to see the best AFK options.

Gemstone Crab
Very AFK

Location: Varlamore (Gemstone Crab area)

AFK Time: 5–10+ minutes per crab (very low attention needed)

Requirements: Access to Varlamore + decent Mining level

Why Great: One of the best current AFK Mining methods. Good XP and gems.

Mining Bone Shards
Very AFK

Location: Varlamore (Bone Shard mining spots)

AFK Time: 3–8 minutes between banking / moving

Requirements: Access to Varlamore + Pickaxe

Why Great: Excellent for Ironmen. Bone shards are useful and the method is very low effort.

Shooting Stars
Extremely AFK

Location: Various (use Star Miners plugin or Discord)

AFK Time: Up to 20–30+ minutes per star

Requirements: Mining level + access to a star

Why Great: One of the most AFK methods in the game. Great for long sessions.

Redwood Trees
Very AFK

Location: Woodcutting Guild (after 90 Woodcutting) or other Redwood spots

AFK Time: 4–8 minutes per tree

Requirements: 90 Woodcutting + Axe

Why Great: Extremely AFK and good XP. One of the best late-game AFK methods.

Forestry (Events)
Semi-AFK

Location: Various trees while doing Forestry events

AFK Time: 2–5 minutes between events

Requirements: Forestry outfit + participation in events

Why Great: Makes regular Woodcutting much more AFK and rewarding.

Karambwan Fishing
Semi-AFK

Location: Karamja (after Tai Bwo Wannai Trio)

AFK Time: 2–4 minutes between banking

Requirements: Tai Bwo Wannai Trio quest + Karambwan vessel

Why Great: Very consistent and decent profit/XP for Ironmen.

Anglerfish
Semi-AFK

Location: Piscatoris Fishing Colony

AFK Time: 3–6 minutes between banking

Requirements: 82 Fishing + access to the colony

Why Great: Good food source + solid AFK training for Ironmen.

Wintertodt
Semi-AFK

Location: Wintertodt Camp

AFK Time: 3–6 minutes per game (with good setup)

Requirements: 50 Firemaking + warm clothing

Why Great: Excellent early AFK method. Great supplies + FM XP.

Guardians of the Rift (Semi-AFK)
Semi-AFK

Location: Guardians of the Rift minigame

AFK Time: 2–5 minutes between runs with good strategy

Requirements: Some Runecrafting + access to the minigame

Why Great: One of the best ways to train Runecrafting with relatively low effort.
