Mining AFK Methods

Copper & Tin Ore
Level 1–15 • Very AFK
Location: Lumbridge Swamp or Varrock East Mine
AFK Time: 3–6 minutes per inventory
Ironman Tip: Mine near a bank or general store. Sell ores or smelt into bars.
Shooting Stars
Level 10+ • Extremely AFK
Best Overall
Location: Various (use Star Miners CC or RuneLite plugin)
AFK Time: 15–30+ minutes per star
Ironman Tip: One of the best AFK methods in the game. Great XP + stardust. Extremely mobile friendly with long AFK times.
Mining Bone Shards
Level 20+ • Very AFK
Great for Mobile
Location: Varlamore (Bone Shard mining spots)
AFK Time: 5–10 minutes
Ironman Tip: Excellent mobile method. Bone shards are useful for Prayer training. Very low effort and consistent.
Amethyst
Level 92 • Very AFK
Best Late Game
Location: Mining Guild (Amethyst crystals)
AFK Time: 6–10 minutes per inventory
Ironman Tip: Best late-game Mining method. Can be High Alched for good profit. Very AFK friendly.
Mining Bone Shards
Level 20+ • Very AFK
Location: Varlamore (Bone Shard mining spots)
AFK Time: 5–10 minutes
Ironman Tip: Bone shards are useful for Prayer training and other content.

Woodcutting AFK Methods

Regular Trees
Level 1–15 • AFK
Location: Lumbridge or Varrock (near banks/general stores)
AFK Time: 2–4 minutes per tree
Ironman Tip: Cut near a general store to sell logs immediately for early cash.
Oak Trees
Level 15+ • Very AFK
Great Early Option
Location: Lumbridge (near general store) or Varrock
AFK Time: 3–6 minutes per tree
Ironman Tip: Excellent early AFK method. Cut in Lumbridge and sell logs to the general store or fletch into arrow shafts on the spot. Very mobile friendly.
Willow Trees
Level 30+ • Very AFK
Location: Draynor Village or Port Sarim
AFK Time: 4–7 minutes
Ironman Tip: Close to a bank. Good for early cash or Fletching XP.
Redwood Trees
Level 90+ • Extremely AFK
Location: Woodcutting Guild
AFK Time: 6–10 minutes
Ironman Tip: One of the best late-game AFK methods. High XP and very low effort.

Fishing AFK Methods

Karambwan Fishing
Level 65+ • Very AFK
Location: Karamja (after Tai Bwo Wannai Trio)
AFK Time: 4–7 minutes between banking
Ironman Tip: One of the best mid-game AFK fishing methods. Great food source and consistent. Very mobile friendly.
Anglerfish
Level 82+ • Very AFK
Location: Piscatoris Fishing Colony
AFK Time: 5–8 minutes
Ironman Tip: Excellent late-game food. Very consistent and low effort once unlocked.

Other AFK Methods

Firemaking

Wintertodt
Level 50+ • Semi-AFK
Location: Wintertodt Camp
AFK Time: 4–8 minutes per game
Ironman Tip: One of the best early AFK methods. Excellent supplies (herbs, seeds, logs, crates).
Bonfires
Level 30+ • Very AFK
Location: Any bank with a fire (e.g. Grand Exchange, Varrock)
AFK Time: 3–6 minutes per inventory
Ironman Tip: Simple and safe. Good for training while doing other things.

Fletching

Fletching Arrow Shafts / Headless Arrows
Level 1–50+ • Extremely AFK
Location: Anywhere with logs (bank standing)
AFK Time: 5–10+ minutes per inventory
Ironman Tip: Very low effort. Great for early Fletching XP while doing other activities.
Stringing Bows
Level 20+ • Very AFK
Location: Bank standing
AFK Time: 4–8 minutes
Ironman Tip: Good profit and XP once you have unstrung bows from drops or other methods.

Smithing

Cannonball Making
Level 35+ • Extremely AFK
Location: Any furnace (Edgeville recommended)
AFK Time: 8–15 minutes per inventory
Ironman Tip: One of the most AFK Smithing methods. Good profit and very low effort.
Blast Furnace (Bars)
Level 30+ • Semi-AFK
Location: Blast Furnace (Keldagrim)
AFK Time: 3–6 minutes between trips (with coal bag)
Ironman Tip: Best XP and profit for mid-high level Smithing. Requires some setup.

Cooking

Cooking Fish (Bank Standing)
Level 1–80+ • Very AFK
Location: Any bank with a range (Lumbridge, Varrock, etc.)
AFK Time: 4–8 minutes per inventory
Ironman Tip: Simple and safe. Good for training while doing other low-attention activities.
