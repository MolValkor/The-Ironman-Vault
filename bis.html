Best-in-Slot & Key Unlocks

Important gear upgrades and Best-in-Slot items for Ironmen, along with exactly what you need to unlock them.

Early Game / Fresh Account Priorities

The most impactful upgrades you should aim for in the first 1–30 hours of an Ironman account.

Rune Scimitar HIGH PRIORITY
Level: 40 Attack  •  Unlock: Kill Lesser Demons in Karamja Dungeon (after starting Dragon Slayer)
Best early melee weapon by a mile. Huge upgrade over steel/iron weapons.
Dragon Dagger
Level: 60 Attack  •  Unlock: Complete Lost City
Excellent special attack weapon for early bossing and PvM. Very strong for Ironmen.
Iban's Staff
Level: 50 Magic & 25 Attack  •  Unlock: Complete Underground Pass
One of the strongest early magic weapons. Built-in high damage spell makes it amazing for Ironmen.
Green D'hide Body
Level: 40 Ranged & 40 Defence  •  Unlock: Complete Heroes' Quest
Big ranged defence and accuracy upgrade. Worth doing Heroes' Quest relatively early.
Amulet of Strength / Power
Unlock: Buy from Port Sarim or GE (after unlocking)
Simple but very effective early melee boost. Easy to get and makes a noticeable difference.
Steel / Black Armour
Unlock: Buy from shops or kill monsters that drop it
Best early defensive armour before you can wear Rune. Black armour is surprisingly good.

Melee Gear

Rune Platebody
Level: 40 Defence  •  Unlock: Complete Dragon Slayer
One of the biggest early defensive upgrades. Essential for mid-game content.
Dragon Defender
Unlock: Warrior's Guild (requires 130 combined Attack + Strength)
Best off-hand for most melee training. Huge accuracy bonus.
Barrows Gloves
Unlock: Recipe for Disaster (full questline)
Best-in-slot gloves for a very long time. Major goal for most Ironmen.

Ranged Gear

Green D'hide Body
Unlock: Complete Heroes' Quest (or buy from GE after unlocking)
Solid early-mid game ranged body. Much better than leather.
Rune Crossbow
Unlock: Complete Dwarf Cannon quest + 61 Ranged
Excellent early ranged weapon. Very useful for many bosses and Slayer.

Accessories & Capes

Fire Cape
Unlock: Complete Fight Caves (Jad)
Best-in-slot cape for a long time. Huge defensive and strength bonus.
Ardougne Cloak
Unlock: Complete Ardougne Easy Diary
Very useful teleport + thieving bonus. Great early diary reward.

Magic Gear

Iban's Staff
Unlock: Complete Underground Pass + 50 Magic
Excellent early magic weapon with built-in high damage spell. Very strong for Ironmen.
Mystic Robes
Unlock: Various (Mystic hat from Slayer, top/bottom from shops or drops)
Solid mid-game magic armour. Big upgrade over basic robes.
Occult Necklace
Unlock: Smoke Dungeon (requires 93 Slayer for superior nechryael)
Best-in-slot magic damage necklace for a very long time.

Utility & Important Unlocks

Fighter Torso
Unlock: Barbarian Assault (requires some team coordination)
Best-in-slot body for melee training. Huge strength bonus. Worth the grind.
Void Knight Equipment
Unlock: Pest Control (requires 40+ combat stats)
Excellent hybrid gear. Very strong for many mid-game activities and bossing.
Dramen / Lunar Staff
Unlock: Lost City (Dramen) → Lunar Diplomacy (Lunar)
Required for Fairy Ring access. One of the most important quality-of-life unlocks in the game.
Proselyte Armour
Unlock: Slug Menace quest
Best early Prayer armour. Great for Prayer training and early bossing.
