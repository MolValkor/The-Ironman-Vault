Efficient Early Quest Order

This recommended order respects level requirements, gives big XP rewards early, and minimizes painful travel across the map.

1. Cook's Assistant — Very fast starter in Lumbridge.
2. Rune Mysteries — Unlocks rune access.
3. Waterfall Quest — Massive early combat XP (do this early!).
4. Tree Gnome Village → The Grand Tree — Grouped for travel efficiency.
5. Lost City + Fairytale Series — Unlocks Zanaris and Fairy Rings.
6. Recipe for Disaster — Major goal. Work toward Barrows Gloves.
