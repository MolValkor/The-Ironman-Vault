Useful Combat Training (Ironman Focus)

Monsters worth killing on an Ironman for useful drops (resources, alchables, gear). Safe spot information included, especially useful for HCIM.

Early Game (Useful Drops)

Sand Crabs / Ammonite Crabs
Location: Fossil Island / Crabclaw Isle
Safe Spot: Stand behind rocks or use the small peninsula spots. Limited aggression range.
Extremely safe and AFK-friendly. Great for early combat training on HCIM.
Hill Giants
Location: Edgeville Dungeon or Giants' Plateau
Safe Spot: Use the safespot behind the pillar in Edgeville Dungeon (very reliable).
Useful Drops: Big bones, limpwurt roots, runes, herbs
Moss Giants
Location: Varrock Sewers or Wilderness (safest in sewers)
Safe Spot: Use the safespot near the entrance in Varrock Sewers (behind the pipe area).
Useful Drops: Herbs, seeds, mossy keys, runes
Flesh Crawlers
Location: Stronghold of Security (2nd floor)
Safe Spot: Multiple safespots available using the pillars and corners.
Useful Drops: Early runes, essence, herbs, seeds
Experiments
Location: Experiments cave (near Observatory)
Safe Spot: Multiple reliable safespots using walls and corners.
Useful Drops: Good herb drops, decent combat XP
Chaos Druids
Location: Edgeville Dungeon or Taverley Dungeon
Safe Spot: Very safe safespot in Taverley Dungeon near entrance.
Useful Drops: High quantity of herbs (excellent for early Herblore)

Mid Game (Strong Resources)

Gargoyles
Location: Slayer Tower (requires 75 Slayer)
Safe Spot: Use the safespot near the entrance or behind pillars.
Useful Drops: Granite maul, runes, herbs, seeds, alchables
Nechryael
Location: Slayer Tower (requires 80 Slayer)
Safe Spot: Safespot near the entrance area works well.
Useful Drops: Seeds, herbs, runes, noted items
Dust Devils
Location: Smoke Dungeon (requires 65 Slayer + facemask)
Safe Spot: Multiple good safespots available in the dungeon.
Useful Drops: Herbs, seeds, noted drops, good alchables

Mid Game (Good Resources)

Chaos Druids
Location: Edgeville Dungeon or Taverley Dungeon
Safe Spot: Use the safespot in Taverley Dungeon near the entrance (very safe).
Useful Drops: High quantity of herbs (great for early Herblore)
Experiments
Location: Experiments cave (near Observatory)
Safe Spot: Multiple reliable safespots using the walls and corners.
Useful Drops: Good herb drops and decent combat XP
About Safe Spots
Most safe spot information above is based on common reliable locations. For visual diagrams of exact tile positions, visit the monster's page on the OSRS Wiki — they usually have excellent safespot images and maps.
