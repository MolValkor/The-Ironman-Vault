Money Making Hub

Ironman-friendly money makers organized by game stage. GP/hr estimates are approximate and can vary.

Early Game (Levels 1–60)

Extremely Fresh Accounts (Right After Tutorial Island)

Killing Chickens
GP/hr: 50k – 150k (early)
Location: Lumbridge chicken coop
Ironman Note: Collect feathers (sell or use for fishing) + raw chicken. Cook and eat or sell. Very good for brand new accounts.
Mining Copper & Tin
GP/hr: 80k – 200k
Location: Lumbridge Swamp or Varrock East Mine
Ironman Note: Mine near a bank or general store. Sell ores or smelt into bronze bars for extra profit.
Woodcutting Regular Trees
GP/hr: 50k – 120k
Location: Lumbridge or near general stores
Ironman Note: Cut trees and sell logs directly to the general store. Simple and effective for the first few hours.
Fishing Shrimp + Cooking
GP/hr: 60k – 150k
Location: Lumbridge river
Ironman Note: Fish shrimp and cook them on the nearby range. Good food + small profit while training two skills.
Early Thieving (Men/Women)
GP/hr: 40k – 100k
Location: Lumbridge or any town
Ironman Note: Pickpocket men and women for coins. Very low requirement and decent for the first few levels.

Slightly More Established Early Game

Wintertodt
GP/hr: 300k – 600k+
Requirements: 50 Firemaking
Ironman Note: Excellent supplies (herbs, seeds, logs, crates). One of the best early methods.
Blackjacking (Thieving)
GP/hr: 400k – 700k
Requirements: 45+ Thieving
Ironman Note: Best early consistent GP. Use food and do it near a bank.

Mid Game (Levels 60–90)

Herb Runs + Bird House Runs
GP/hr: 500k – 1M+ (passive)
Requirements: 50+ Farming & Hunter
Ironman Note: Extremely good passive income. Do these daily.
Barrows
GP/hr: 800k – 1.5M+
Requirements: Decent combat + Prayer
Ironman Note: Great for runes, bolt racks, and potential big ticket items.
Slayer (Abyssal Demons, etc.)
GP/hr: 600k – 1.2M
Requirements: 85+ Slayer recommended
Ironman Note: Good XP + consistent profit from alchables and uniques.

Late Game (90+)

Vorkath
GP/hr: 2M – 4M+
Requirements: 90+ Combat + good gear
Ironman Note: One of the best consistent late-game money makers.
Chambers of Xeric / Tombs of Amascut
GP/hr: 3M – 8M+ (scaled)
Requirements: Strong gear + team or solo experience
Ironman Note: Best money makers in the game when done efficiently.

High Alchemy Methods (Great for Ironmen)

These methods combine skilling + High Alchemy for consistent profit with low competition.

Fletch + High Alch Longbows
GP/hr: 400k – 800k
Method: Fletch yew/magic longbows → High Alch them
Ironman Note: Very popular for new-mid Ironmen. Easy to do while watching something.
Craft + High Alch Dragonhide Armor
GP/hr: 600k – 1.2M
Method: Kill dragons → Tan hides → Craft bodies/chaps → High Alch
Ironman Note: Green d'hide bodies are especially good. Very reliable profit.
Amethyst Mining + High Alch
GP/hr: 700k – 1.1M
Requirements: 92 Mining
Ironman Note: Mine amethyst → Craft into arrowtips/bolt tips and alch where profitable.
Blast Furnace + High Alch Rune Items
GP/hr: 800k – 1.5M+
Method: Smith rune platebodies at Blast Furnace → High Alch
Ironman Note: One of the best consistent mid-late game methods when done efficiently.
